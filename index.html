About Me

I'm originally from Lompoc, a small town on the central coast of California. After graduating high school, I moved to Oregon to attend college and enjoy all of the outdoor activities that Oregon has to offer.

In college, I had planned to major in computer science, but as an out-of-state student, tuition became a difficult hurdle to overcome. Thankfully, discovering Thinkful has helped me respark my passion for becoming a web developer. Most of my past jobs in the service industry have made me appreciate and look forward to working in a team setting to tackle a project or task!
